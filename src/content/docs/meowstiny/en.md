# Meowstiny: User Manual

**Meowstiny** is a daily, one-tap health diary for your cat. It's built for owners caring for cats with chronic conditions — cystitis/FLUTD, kidney disease (CKD), diabetes, IBD — who need to keep an eye on daily condition. Log fast, see the trend at a glance, and show your vet a single clear report.

> No login. All records stay on your device. Available in Korean, English, and Japanese.

## 1. Getting Started

There's nothing to sign up for. Open the app, add your cat's profile (name and a photo are enough), and you're ready to log. If you have more than one cat, you can add a profile for each.

## 2. Daily Logging

The home screen is built around the daily log. With one or two taps you can record:

- **Pee** — color, condition, and count
- **Poop** — condition and count
- **Weight** — track changes over time
- **Respiratory rate** — see [section 6](#6-respiratory-rate)

The goal is to make logging fast enough that it actually fits into your day.

## 3. Medication & Reminders

Add your cat's medications and check them off as you give them. Turn on **reminder notifications** so doses arrive on time — useful for cats on long-term treatment where adherence matters.

> If notifications aren't arriving, make sure notifications are enabled for Meowstiny in your iPhone's Settings → Notifications.

## 4. Calendar & Statistics

The **unified calendar** shows every record by date, so weeks of small notes become a readable pattern. The statistics view summarizes the signals that matter for chronic care:

- 7-day summary
- Severity trend
- Medication adherence
- Weight trend

## 5. The Vet Report

Tap to generate a single-image **report** you can show at the clinic. Choose the period:

- Since your last visit
- Past 2 weeks
- Past 1 month
- Past 3 months

The report summarizes abnormal urination, blood, and weight loss, and includes your own notes. **Save** it to your photos or **share** it directly with your vet.

## 6. Respiratory Rate

Measure your cat's resting respiratory rate in the app and log it. Resting respiratory rate is an early signal worth watching in cats with heart or breathing concerns.

## 7. Cat Profiles

Keep a separate profile for each cat. Switch between them to log and review each cat's history independently.

## 8. Data & Privacy

Meowstiny requires **no account and no login**. Every record is stored **on your device** only.

> This app helps you keep home observations and is not a substitute for medical diagnosis. If you suspect a health problem, always consult your veterinarian.
